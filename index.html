Forró Federation

Helping event promoters and curators reach their audicence with little effort

2024 (in progress)

What would you do if you moved to a different city and wanted to dance Forró? In the Forró community, we often hear how difficult it is to keep track of what’s happening, especially if you’re new.

As part of the efforts in providing support for the community professionals, the European Forró Federation wanted to first find out more about the work of people who publish events and curate event listings. This in the context in which I was invited to take over this Research project.

As a volunteer, I am currently researching event publishing and event list curation in the community. The project involves qualitative and quantitative research, as well as a workshop and presentation to stakeholders.

Type of research generative
Methods survey, user interviews
Artifacts storyboard, user journey map, slide report
Impact in progress

Sparkly

Creating a harmonious collaboration experience in shared living spaces

2021

Sparkly is a course project I completed while doing the UX Research and Strategy course at Design Lab. I chose to do research and design the concept of an app that would help people who live in a shared flat organize chores and cleaning tasks. As part of the course curriculum, we would start with exploratory research and work our way to designing user flows and a landing page. I worked on this project with the help of a course mentor.

Type of research generative
Methods user interviews
Artifacts personas, affinity diagrams, empathy maps, storyboards, user journey map, feature matrix, user flows
Impact course completion
